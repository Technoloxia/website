FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS


  • Who can attend?
    If you are presently in high-school (grades 9-12), then you can apply. If you are an undergrad or above and want to get involved, contact us to apply for being a mentor or a speaker.
  • Is this a hackathon?
    Nope. It is not a hackathon. We are, as of now, referring to Technoloxia as a "tech-fest", you can expect workshops, competitions (aka "mini hackathon-esque" events) and a lot more!
  • Are there any pre-requisites?
    Nope, again. If you love technology and computer science, or even are just getting started, then you are more than welcome to apply!
  • What are the fees like?
    Null. Nada. Zero. We'll provide you with free food, sponsor swag, hardware and software to keep you fueled all the three days
  • Is there a selection process/criteria?
    Since this is a free event, seats are pretty limited. The applications are not open yet but we suggest you keep an eye on this website and apply as early as you can.
  • I don't want to attend. I want to speak/mentor/sponsor...
    Feel free to reach out to us at any of our social pages, or the email address provided below and we'd be happy to help you!
  • What if I am in grade 6 and above and want to attend?
    We are planning some workshops and events for younger students - stay tuned to this website for the details.
  • Is there anything at stake?
    Yes! There is something for everyone - there are going to be multiple competitions each day where you can compete and win money, hardware and software!
  • My question is not listed here
    Reach out to us at this.is.us@technoloxia.tech and we'd be more than happy to help!
